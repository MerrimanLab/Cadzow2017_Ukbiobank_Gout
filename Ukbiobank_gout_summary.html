Gout Numbers

There are 7961 total posssible gout cases and 404935 total potential controls from a total dataset of 502655 people. Of the gout cases, there are:

  • 1197 cases with either a primary or secondary hospital diagnosis (using ICD-10)

  • 6027 cases as defined by the Winnard et al 2012 criteria (hospital diagnosis or on gout drugs)
